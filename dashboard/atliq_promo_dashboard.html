💡 Key Findings — Executive Summary
5 actionable insights for business decision-making
Recruiter / Stakeholder View
1️⃣
BOGOF Delivers Highest Volume Growth
Buy-One-Get-One-Free achieved +270% growth and added +157K incremental units — the single most effective promo type. Ideal for high-frequency grocery items.
2️⃣
Cashback Effective for Premium Products
₹500 Cashback drove +183% growth (+41K units). Best suited for high-basket items like Home Essential Combos. Psychologically drives perceived value.
3️⃣
25% OFF is Counterproductive — Avoid
25% discount resulted in −13% growth (−5.7K units). Customers may perceive this as insufficient — neither deep enough to excite nor margin-preserving. Discontinue this tier.
4️⃣
Sankranti Campaign Outperforms Diwali
Sankranti grew 155.3% vs Diwali's 66.2%. Despite fewer baseline units (99K vs 110K), Sankranti yielded 252K units after — a +153K lift vs Diwali's +73K.
5️⃣
Tier-1 Cities Drive the Majority of Sales — Focus Marketing There
Bengaluru (105K), Chennai (83K), and Hyderabad (69K) together account for 58% of all post-promo units. Mid-tier cities (CBE, MYS, VZG) show moderate potential. Mangalore, Vijayawada, Trivandrum have low response and need store-level investigation before further investment.
